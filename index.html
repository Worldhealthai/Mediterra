Crafted by Nature. Refined Through Experience. Elevated by Purpose.

Mediterra Mussel Farm is a family-owned aquaculture enterprise rooted in one of Greece's most exceptional marine landscapes.

Here, powerful natural currents carve their own rhythm, renewing the waters and shaping the environment in which our mussels grow.

We combine meticulous care, disciplined work, and a deep respect for the sea to offer premium Mediterranean mussels to select wholesale partners in Athens and throughout Greece.

Our philosophy is simple:

Quality without compromise. Excellence without shortcuts.

The Artemisium Strait – A Sea That Remembers

Between North Evia and the Greek mainland lies a strait known not only for its clarity and depth, but also for its quiet historical significance.

These waters once witnessed ancient maritime passages and events that helped shape the story of the Mediterranean.

Today, the same natural currents continue their timeless motion — strong, clean, and continuously renewing.

This remarkable marine environment is defined by:

  • Continuous natural movement
  • High oxygen levels
  • Reliable water purity
  • Rich mineral and nutrient flow
  • A stable, balanced ecosystem

Every mussel we harvest carries the imprint of this place: deep, resilient, refined, and unmistakably true to its origin.

Cultivation Guided by Nature, Perfected by Mastery

Our mussels are raised using the long-line method, allowing them to grow suspended in open, moving water — as close to their natural environment as possible.

We follow a disciplined, responsible approach:

  • Natural growth with no artificial feeding
  • Daily oversight of water conditions
  • Selective harvesting at peak maturity
  • Clean, respectful handling across all stages

This is not mass aquaculture.

This is sea-crafted precision, shaped by dedication and respect for the environment that sustains us.
